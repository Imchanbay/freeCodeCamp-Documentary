B. J. Habibie

A Scientist and A President of Indonesia

Here's a time line of B. J. Habibie's life:

  • 1936 - Born in Parepare, Dutch East Indies
  • 1954 - Studies aviation and aerospace at the Techische Hogeschool Delft
  • 1955 - Continues his study at the Technische Hochshule Aachen due to political reasons
  • 1960 - Recieves his engineer's degree with the title Diplome-ingenieur
  • 1962 - Returns to Indonesia for three months and married with Hasri Ainun
  • 1965 - Delivers his dissertation in aerospace engineering and recieves the grade of "very good" and gets the title Doktoringenieur
  • 1974 - Works for Messerschmitt on the development of the Airbus A-300B aircraft and gets promoted to vice-president of the company
  • 1974 - Gets recruited to return by the president of Indonesia at the time to industrialize and develop the country
  • 1978 - Becomes a State Sinister of Research and Technology of Indonesia
  • 1980 - Becomes CEO of the new state-owned enterprise Industri Pesawat Terbang Nurtanio
  • 1998 - Becomes the 7th Vice President of Indonesia and in the same year, he becomes the 3rd President of Indonesia
  • 2019 - Dies at the age of 83
